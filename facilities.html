Facilites

RC South provides its residents with the following amneties:

Library

RC South has its own private library with a lots of book where you can study privately by yourself or in a group.

Computer Lab

RC South has three Macs and five Computers where residents can surf and work on their stuff .

Ping Pong tables

RC South has two ping pong tables where residents can enjoy and refresh.

Cafeteria

Rc South has its own cafeteria in the ground floor so the residents do not have to leave the buildings for food.

Gym

Residents in RC South has access to the gym where they can workout without leaving the building.

Kitchen

RC South has a fully functioning kitchen with kitchen supplies where residents can c cook as they please.
