Welcome to RC South!

The Residential College is unique among residential housing units at the University of Mississippi. This 466-bed co-ed facility cultivates a culture of student engagement setting it apart from other housing options. Most important, students have the option of remaining at the RC all four years while completing their undergraduate education.
